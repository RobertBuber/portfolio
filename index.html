Robert Buber is a creative who uses art, design & motion to create emotive experiences. his services include brand strategy, creative ideation, campaign concepts, design for motion & film, silly little games, photo direction & shortform scriptwriting.

based in Detroit, his mind wanders, helping industry titans, centuries-old institutions, sprinting start-ups & rebellious individuals.

at the moment, you'll find him sitting in a movie theater, reading in a cafe, or pushing his clients' work a little further.
