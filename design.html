Art director in search of something great.

Along the way, Buber has worked with brands of all sizes. He's designed campaigns, brands, commercials; all sorts of stuff. He looks for ways to help people think, feel, & laugh.

Projects are concept first. Every creative decision is based on an idea found by research across a ton of fields. Once the ideas are in place, then he looks for tools or partners to bring them to life in the strongest way possible.

He strives for excellence & often misses. But his clients are happy, the work is effective, & we all enjoy the process.
