I worry that everything

I've got will never

be Enough

I chase perfection, assuming I won't find it. I just feel that talent is the only thing one can ever own.

it's led me out of detroit & into some incredible places. From coast to coast & abroad, my thinking challenges teams to push thier artistry. In the daytime, I've helped brands like Ford, Mazda, & Jeep tell more meaningful stories. I've pushed empathy & emotion into institutions like the U.S. Army & AARP. I've kicked back for folks like Bud Light, Budweiser, & Jim Beam.

Once the sun sets & agencies close shop, I march on – prowling museums for ideas or sitting in life drawing for hours or babbling on in french class – looking for those parts of life that help me shine a little brighter & push my talent a little further.
