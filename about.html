About

A Picture is worth 1,000 worlds

Designer. Writer. Artist. Dreamer.
Whatever it is, I imagine. I think of forms that people, places, & ideas can take. I like to squish reality around. I try to capture feelings & desires with pictures. Every piece of work is like a bridge between me & the outside world.

Waste not, work not

I don't like downtime. I read constantly. I study constantly. I’m constantly writing, drawing, or designing. I dream in Photoshop. I continue to learn of new tricks, tools, & trades. I find opportunities for challenge, collaboration, & improvement. I don't tire often. I feel like there's much more beyond the horizon.

Getting Home Sick

Once Covid ends, I’m excited to hop back in the pool & get back to swimming. I also plan on renewing my museum & theater memberships. I’ll probably grab a library card as well. I’ve missed figure drawing, so it'll be fun to find another class. Like many others, I don't enjoying sitting inside all the time.
