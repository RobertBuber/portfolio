Detroit Bikes
Detroit Bikes wanted ads cool enough to tear out and hang on the wall. For that, I made a series of hand made ads, pulling ideas from the values of the company (All-American, Hand-built, simple). Winner of AdCraft Detroit's Best in Print and Best in Graphic Design.
U.S. Army
We were challenged to change perception of the Army from a home for warriors to an innovative employer of the smart & industrious. I was part of a team handling the concept & art direction for various campaigns mainly in the digital & social space.
Pioneer
Pioneer's newest soybean had performance you couldn't get from a host of otherwise identical brands. With dynamic graphics & clear language, I helped lead a campaign to spell out our product's uniqueness across video, print, and digital spaces.
Bud Light
At a time when people couldn't travel, we brought the vacation to them. This campaign asked hordes of people trapped on zoom to take a day off with Bud Light Seltzer's tropical flavors. I helped design store, bar, & print collateral.
This is Love & Criminal
This is Love & Criminal both bend genre expectations. We figured the advertising should do that. So, we combined the two podcasts into one campaign. After listening to 100s of love & murder stories, I had enough quotes to mix into simple posters.
adidas
A lot of us are great. It's just deep inside. Adidas is the a perfect brand to unlock that greatness every step of the way, whether on the streets or on the field. Self-made concept.
Glad
Glad hadn't adapted as online-shopping millennials moved into houses (filling them with plastic). To help them modernize, I thought of new ways to show Glad's quality, then used 3D to show that in a fun, Instagram-able way. Personal concept.



Brands can get so emotional

I learned that working on Mustang, F-150 & Lincoln. Since beginning my journey at Ford, I've helped brands like Budweiser, Milk Bar, & The United States Army connect to their audience through wit, charm, & vibrancy. I rely on clear strategy, a bit of human nature, & a strong personal vision.

The Idea rules every project. I don't stop until I find that compelling fact, the unexpected twist, or that head-scratching insight that focuses the message or reinforces a brand's beliefs. I look to who the audience wants to be. Once that's in sight, I push myself & my teams to create fresh, refined, & exciting work.

My conceptual thinking & dediction to craft has kicked off memorable campaigns & elevated brands. It's helped win pitches, rack up awards, build an agency, & excite clients. From Detroit, in Chicago, I'm ready to meet you anywhere on Earth.
