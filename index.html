Robert Buber is an art director and designer who excels in the craft of ideas & communications. His work shapes brands, tells stories, & sells products.

His never-say-no attitude took him from a small art college in Detroit to GTB, working on national product launches for Ford & Lincoln. He's currently helping to build Lafayette American, a new creative boutique with over 70 years of advertising experience, from the ground up.

He divides his free time inbetween the museum and the theater – drawing, painting, reading, and photographing along the way. He pulls references from film, music, fashion, architecture, nature, and books. Traveling 2-3 times a year, he's always challenging his own sensiblities, staying cognizant of trends, and pushing himself towards fresher, bolder ideas.
