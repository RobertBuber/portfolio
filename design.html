Brands should make people feel something.

Buber learned this lesson early in his career, working on product launches for Mustang, F-150, & Lincoln. Now, he helps marketing depts., mayors, & founders connect with their audience. He designs brands that look great, communicate clearly, & feel human.

He approaches work with an artists' touch, but creative decisions are built on research. Who are we talking to? What do they like? How can we help? Once we find those connections, then we make something cool.

He's used this approach to conceptualize ad campaigns, design brands, & make fun graphics for the internet. Over the past two years, it's helped win pitches and grow Lafayette American into a 20 person advertising agency with happy clients. He's currently available for new opportunities.
