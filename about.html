A Picture is worth 1,000 worlds

Designer. Writer. Artist. Dreamer.
Whatever it is, I imagine. I invent, I bend, I break, I think, & I think, & I think, & I try to create things that help people see things a little differently. I treat every piece like a brief escape to a distant place.

The 100,000 Hour Rule

Wake up, walk, work, rest, work, walk, sleep. I work to play. I work to relax. I work on vacation. I read books about work. I study the tools, the rules, the history, & future of creativity – looking for ways to make my work just a bit better. It's why I read the ADG instead of the NYT. It's why I got a camera before a couch. It’s why I push myself and my teams whether it's the 100th brief, or the 1,000th project, or the 10,000th hour. I think great things come to the persistent.

What's next

They say the pandemic is the lost year & I disagree. I've never felt more excited. More creative. mMre growth. The Human spirit has always been about finding solutions. Ways to cope. Ways to thrive. Ways to laugh. We shape narratives, shift focus, & provide escape. It doesn't matter if it's on a couch, a cafe, or a conference room. Stay healthy & keep pushing.
