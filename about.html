A Picture is worth 1,000 worlds

Designer. Writer. Artist. Dreamer.
Whatever it is, I imagine. I invent, I bend, I break, I think, & I think, & I think, & I try to create things that help people see things a little differently. I treat every piece like a brief escape to a distant place.

Waste not, work not

I don't like downtime. I read constantly. I study constantly. I’m constantly writing, sketching, or shooting. I dream in Photoshop, I think in Illustrator. I continue to learn of new tricks, tools, & trades. I find opportunities for challenge, collaboration, & improvement. I'm always pushing. There's so much more beyond the horizon.
