D.D.O.T., Brand
Detroit hadn't modernized its Dept. of Transportation graphics since the seventies. To visualize that, we worked with the mayor, transportation department, & community members on a new logo, bus wraps, fare cards, & brand guidelines.
Pioneer, Motion Graphics
We went to capture the science behind Pioneer's latest product. To match the excitement of our geneticists, I created an animated visual language inspired by bar signs, giving our creatives freedom without losing consistency between spots.
U.S. Army, Styleframes
I was brought in to design a new look for a video campaign defining different disciplines within the Army. The approach for each script (conceptually & visually) centered around carefully-researched passions our audience has.
Detroit is Sci-Fi,Community Event
Created the look & feel for various posters & posts promoting the Museum of Contemporary Art - Detroit's creative workshop in Detroit. We focused on how children literally transform into different creatures as they play.
Budweiser, Neon
Neon bar signs from a larger Summer campaign developed for Budweiser designed to appeal to both National & Local vendors across various product lines.
Alphaville, Posters
A series of large-format posters inspired from Jean-Luc Godard's film, Alphaville. Each, a reflection on the conflict between Humans and Machines.
Museum of Contemporary Art, Detroit
Title cards for the art film, "Is My Mind A Machine Gun?" by KESSWA & Shigeto. Designed to reflect the free-flowing intention behind her music & match the eclectic nature of the video.
Core City, Brand
A shape-shifting brand system inspired by retro arcades, designed to foreshadow the once-overlooked neigborhood would be the center of many fun, community-rooted improvements.
