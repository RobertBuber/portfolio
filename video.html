U.S. Army
Our mission was to show the Army as an innovative, inclusive employer for America's strivers. Armed with a tiny team & endless inspiration, I designed over 100 videos, gifs, slideshows, infocards, & quizzes for the Army's social accounts.
Corteva
Snippets from a long-form technical video where a no-nonsense farmer (our customer) who slices complicated science into bite-sized "what's in it for me?" pieces. I led development of this piece from conception to edit & color.
Milk Bar

BorgWarner
