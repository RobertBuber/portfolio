I chase perfection knowing you can never find it. Along the way, I've made plenty of incredible friends & maybe have made one or two things that have made the world a more beautiful place.

that journey has led me from detroit to some incredible places & back. Working coast to coast & overseas, my thinking has helped teams create better work, faster. I've told meaningful stories for brands like Ford, Mazda & Jeep. I've poured empathy & emotion into institutions like the U.S. Army & AARP. I've kicked back & created for folks like Bud Light, Budweiser & Jim Beam.

When agencies close shop for the day, my search for perfection continues – prowling museums for ideas or sitting in life drawing for hours or babbling on in french class – looking for those parts of life that help me shine a little brighter & push my talent a little further.
