Cafe Confidante

A lover of fast-talk over slow espressos

Job Description

Discussions of the trends & times of our exciting world. Digressions, interjections, & Freudian slips encouraged.

Qualifications

Our ideal candidate will be unfocused - easily distracted by the subtleties of sights & sounds. They must have a knowledge of Chicago’s finest cafe’s & experience visiting them. It’ll be considered a bonus if this person reads well between science, news, fiction, & history.

Responsibilities

This is a fast-paced social role & applicants must be able to not only hold conversation, but endure icy silences & a fair amount of rakishness. Drinking coffee or tea is required & the applicant’s usual order should not over-encumber the folks preparing the drink. Applicants are expected to be strong planners & capable of coordinating times & places.

Museum Amuser

For curious minds with wandering eyes

Job Description

Be educated enough to know the difference between Modern Expressionism & Post-Modern Impressionism, but wise enough to keep it to yourself.

Qualifications

Those fit to be an amuser probably already have a museum membership. If not, the Head of House can donate his own. This candidate’s sense of curiousness should exceed their common sense & they should have an excess of both. We expect this person to be well-traveled, well-informed, & extraordinarily critical.

Responsibilities

The House is looking for strong vision, preferably 20-20 (corrected or natural). This person must be able to spot extraordinarily mundane details & imbue them with spectacular insight. Original thinking, paired with the commensurate amount of wit & charm, will be requested. Folks will be expected to cross-reference artworks across time, history, & psychological contexts.

Flanuer au Nature

Be a person whom roses stop to admire

Job Description

A variety of projects to survey, enjoy, & complain about the world, real & imagined.

Qualifications

This is an entry-level position & such, there are no necessary qualifications beyond desire.

Responsibilities

This role will adapt to those who accept it, but there are a few common traits to consider. The ability to plan a day in the park & organize the necessary means is important. This person will also be expected to generate & maintain a vibe. Given the unpredictable nature of the outdoors, candidates may have to lift heavy weights, survive danger, & quickly solve problems.
